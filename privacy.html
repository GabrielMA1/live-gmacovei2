Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Gabriel Macovei ("I", "me", "we") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit gmacovei.com (the "Site") or contact me through the Site.

1. Information I collect

I collect only the information needed to respond to your inquiry and operate the Site:

  • Information you provide — name, email, company, phone (optional), service needed, budget range, and message submitted via the contact form.
  • Technical information — anonymous data such as browser type, device, referring page, and approximate location, collected through standard web logs and cookies.

2. How I use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss potential work.
  • To improve the content, performance, and user experience of the Site.
  • To meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Form submissions

The contact form is processed through Formspree. When you submit the form, the data you enter is transmitted to Formspree and forwarded to my email. Please review Formspree's privacy policy for more information on their handling of data.

4. Cookies

The Site may use a minimal number of cookies and similar technologies for essential functionality and basic analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as expected if you do.

5. Third-party services

The Site may link to third-party websites (such as rielart.com and myspotix.com). I am not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

6. Data retention

I retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to communicate with you and fulfill any resulting engagement, then archive or delete them in line with reasonable business practice.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information I hold about you. To exercise these rights, email contact@gmacovei.com.

8. Security

I take reasonable measures to protect submitted information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You provide information at your own risk.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact me at contact@gmacovei.com.
