About

A founder-operator helping businesses modernize growth.

I'm Gabriel Macovei — a digital strategist and entrepreneur with 5+ years building ventures at the intersection of digital innovation and local marketing. I've founded and operate two companies that solve very different sides of the same problem: how modern businesses get found, get trusted, and get customers.

Through RielArt, I help startups and small businesses grow with custom websites, brand identity, paid campaigns, and AI-powered operations. With Spotix, I run a direct-mail marketing system that puts local Toronto businesses physically into 10,000 homes — no algorithms in the way.

Working as both an operator and a consultant gives me an unfair advantage: I don't just hand off strategy decks. I build the systems, run the ads, ship the workflows, and stay accountable to the numbers.

SP
Founder & OperatorLocal Marketing

Spotix

What it is: A direct-mail advertising service that delivers postcards to 10,000 Toronto homes every month.

Problem solved: Local businesses were over-paying for noisy digital ads with shrinking organic reach. Spotix puts their offer physically in the customer's hand.

Strategy: Simple "spot" sizes (Mini, Compact, Standard, Prime), in-house design help, postal-code targeting, and a productized monthly delivery model — no algorithms, no tech skills required from the client.
